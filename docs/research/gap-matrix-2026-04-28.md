**Tier 1 Direct Breeders:** Most are thin sites (under 1,000 words), WooCommerce storefronts, no CITES/USDA disclosure, no state pages, red-flag payment methods (CashApp, PayPal F&F). Strongest by content: **exoticparrotpetstore.com** (pricing visible, phone, blog, named bird listings, star ratings). Most aggressive on state targeting: **africangrayparrotsforsale.com**. Strongest founder story + shipping page: **afrobirdsfarm.com**. Only competitor with USDA + CITES language: **birdsforsales.com**.

**Tier 2 Aggregators:** Dominate via URL volume (BirdsNow covers all 50 states via search filter pages). CAG must win on trust, depth, and authority per page — not breadth.

**Tier 3 Informational:** Spruce Pets is the dominant authority but does not sell birds. African Grey-specific gaps exist because Rational Parrot and Parrot Website have no dedicated African Grey nav category.

**Biggest CAG Advantage Available:** No competitor in the Tier 1 set combines (a) USDA/CITES compliance language, (b) state landing pages at scale, (c) a comprehensive care guide library, and (d) DNA testing trust signals on one site. The first Tier 1 breeder site to do all four owns the trust layer that every current competitor lacks.
