## Authority Signals
- USDA license: Not at platform level; individual breeders may list USDA numbers on profiles
- CITES permit: Not mentioned at platform level
- Vet affiliation: None at platform level
- Founding year: ~2010
- Physical address: No single address
- Phone number: Contact routed through breeder profiles
- Trust badges: "Verified Breeder" badge program on some profiles — key differentiator vs other aggregators
- Reviews: Star ratings on breeder profile pages; count varies per breeder

## Key Insight
BirdBreeders' "Verified Breeder" badge creates a platform-level trust signal, but CAG can counter with superior credentials (USDA AWA license number, CITES captive-bred documentation, named avian vet) that aggregate platforms cannot offer on behalf of individual breeders — these should be front-and-center on CAG's conversion pages.
