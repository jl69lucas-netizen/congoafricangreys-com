Blog Hub — Card Design Options

Same content, three visual treatments, real hero images from the live posts. Pick one option (or mix: e.g. B featured row + A grid). The chosen card style replaces today's text-only cards in all four hub sections (21 cards total — 9 blog posts + 12 interior spokes).

Option A — Image-Top Editorial (Recommended)

16:9 hero thumbnail, category chip overlaid, title + excerpt below

Why recommended: matches the Direction-D editorial idiom and the bird-card pattern buyers already know from /available/; 16:9 crops every hero cleanly (even portrait ones); the overlay chip saves a text row so cards stay compact. Trade-off: heaviest image treatment of the three — mitigated by dedicated 420w q60 thumbnails (~8–15KB each, lazy-loaded).
