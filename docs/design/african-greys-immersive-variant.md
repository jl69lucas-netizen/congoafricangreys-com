# Design.md for Congo African Greys - Immersive Variant

## Brand Identity
A premium, trustworthy family aviary specializing in African Grey parrots. The brand balances professional USDA-licensed credentials with the warmth of a family-raised environment.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green (#2D5A43)**: Represents nature, health, and stability. Used for primary backgrounds and headers.
- **Secondary Orange (#E67451)**: High-contrast call-to-action color. Used for primary buttons and highlighting "Proven with Trust."
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF)**: Used for high-readability text on dark backgrounds and clean whitespace.
- **Translucent White (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.15))**: Used for glassmorphic containers and subtle borders.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display or Merriweather) for a classic, authoritative, and established feel.
- **Body & Buttons**: Sans-serif font (e.g., Inter or Montserrat) for modern readability and clean interaction.

## Visual Style
- **Immersive Imagery**: Full-bleed background photography featuring the birds in naturalistic settings.
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects (backdrop-filter: blur) to create depth while maintaining context of the background image.
- **Clean Iconography**: Thin-stroke white icons for credentials (USDA, CITES, DNA, Health).
- **Smooth Curves**: Large border-radii on buttons and card containers for an organic, friendly feel.

## Layout Components
1. **Header**: Transparent or semi-transparent with a clean logo and centered navigation.
2. **Hero Content**: Centered content block with a primary heading, descriptive subhead, and two distinct CTAs (Primary Orange, Secondary Outlined).
3. **Trust Badges**: A horizontal row of outlined cards highlighting key certifications.
