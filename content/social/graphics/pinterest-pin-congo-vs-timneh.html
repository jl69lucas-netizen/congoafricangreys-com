Congo vs Timneh:
Which Grey Is Right
for You?

We raise both here at C.A.Gs. Here's the honest difference before you choose.

Congo
  • ✔ Larger, light-grey body, red tail
  • ✔ The classic, famous talker
  • ✔ Bolder, more sensitive
  • ✔ $1,700–$2,500
Timneh
  • ✔ Smaller, darker, maroon tail
  • ✔ Talks early, very steady
  • ✔ Often calmer for first-timers
  • ✔ $1,500–$1,600
