# PART 2 — PAID ADS (only Facebook, Instagram, YouTube)

> Why only these three: Meta (FB+IG) is where your 40–65 buyers + the best targeting are; YouTube reaches researchers. Pinterest/TikTok ads aren't worth a small budget. **Run organic for a few weeks first** so the pixel/audience has data.

## Before any ad — one-time setup
- **Meta Business Suite** (business.facebook.com) → connect the FB Page + IG account.
- **Install the Meta Pixel** on congoafricangreys.com (so you can see who visits + retarget). Hand this to a dev or @cag-site-hygiene-agent — GA4 `generate_lead` is already wired; the Pixel is separate.
- **Payment method** on the ad account.

## Facebook + Instagram ads (one campaign covers both)
1. **Ads Manager** → Create → **Objective: Traffic** (or Leads). ⚠️ NOT "Sales" — that expects a store + triggers the live-animal filter.
2. **Ad set:**
   - Audience: US · ages **30–65** · interests *African grey parrot, parrots, exotic pets, companion birds*.
   - Placements: Automatic (lets it run on FB + IG feeds, Stories, Reels).
   - Budget: **$5–10/day**. Schedule: run continuously, review after 4–5 days.
3. **Ad:** pick the format (single video / image / carousel) → paste **Ad 1 (anti-scam/trust)** from ad-creative.md → set the **CTA = Learn More** → destination = the scam-guide or for-sale page → Publish (it goes to review ~24h).
4. **A/B:** duplicate the ad, swap in **Ad 2 (brand)**. Let them run; keep the cheaper-per-click one.
5. **Boosting shortcut (simplest):** on the Page, hit **Boost** under your best organic post → same audience/budget → Learn More button. Easier than Ads Manager, slightly less control. Fine to start.

## YouTube ads (Google Ads)
1. **ads.google.com** → New Campaign → **Goal: Website traffic** (or Leads) → **Campaign type: Video**.
2. **Link your YouTube channel** to Google Ads (Tools → Linked accounts).
3. Upload/choose the ad video (the flock clip; ideally the in-stream recut with the payoff in the first 5s).
4. **Format:** Skippable in-stream + In-feed.
5. Audience: US · 30–65 · same interests + "in-market: pets."
6. Headline + description + Final URL from ad-creative.md §B → **Final URL = /african-grey-parrot-for-sale/**.
7. Budget **$5–10/day**. YouTube needs ~1–2 weeks to optimize — be patient.

## Reading results (all platforms)
- Watch **cost-per-click** and **link clicks**, then **form inquiries** on the site.
- Good sign: inquiries rising. Bad sign: spend with no site visits → pause, change the creative (usually the first line/thumbnail).
- Reallocate to whatever brings inquiries cheapest — almost always Meta for a breeder.

## Hard "don'ts" (these get ads rejected or accounts banned)
- ❌ A bird's price or "buy now / DM to buy this bird" *inside the ad*.
- ❌ "Sales/Shopping" objective for live animals.
- ❌ Gift card / Zelle / wire language.
- ❌ 🦜 emoji, "wild-caught," or anything you can't document.
- ✅ DO drive to the website and let the conversation/sale happen there.
