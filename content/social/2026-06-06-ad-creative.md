## A. META ADS (Facebook + Instagram — same Ads Manager)

> One ad set runs on both FB + IG. Objective = **Traffic** or **Leads** (NOT Sales/Catalog — that triggers the animal-sale filter). Field limits: Primary text ~125 chars before "…more"; Headline ≤40 chars; Description ≤30 chars.

### Ad 1 — Anti-Scam / Trust (PRIMARY — best converter)
**Creative:** flock video (buy-african-grey-parrot-near-me.mp4) OR trust-bar.html PNG
**Destination:** https://congoafricangreys.com/how-to-avoid-african-grey-parrot-scams/
**CTA button:** Learn More

- **Primary text:**
  Lost a deposit to a fake "breeder" — or scared you might? Here's how to tell a real African Grey breeder from a scam: real CITES Appendix I papers, a USDA license you can verify, DNA + vet certificates, and a breeder who'll get on a call first. Here at C.A.Gs we're a family aviary in Midland, TX, raising documented greys since 2014.
- **Headline:** Spot a Real Grey Breeder
- **Description:** USDA-licensed · since 2014
- **Why it's policy-safe:** it sells *trust/education*, not a bird.

### Ad 2 — Brand / "Meet our aviary" (TRUST, softer)
**Creative:** flock video or a clean Congo portrait
**Destination:** https://congoafricangreys.com/about/

## B. YOUTUBE ADS (Google Ads — video campaign)

> YouTube ads = Google Ads, not the YouTube app. Two useful formats below. Same policy rule: trust/education, drive to site. Skip until FB/IG are proven — YouTube needs more budget to learn.

### Format 1 — Skippable in-stream (plays before other videos; first 5s are everything)
**Video:** flock trust clip (buy-african-grey-parrot-near-me.mp4) — recut so the strongest "hand steps up" moment is in the first 5 seconds.
**On-screen first 5s text:** "What a hand-raised African Grey actually looks like."

**Description:** Documented, DNA-sexed, shipped nationwide.

## C. Targeting + budget (applies to all three platforms)

- **Audience:** US, ages **30–65** (exclude under 25 — can't afford a $1,500+ grey). Interests: *African grey parrot, parrot, exotic pets, bird ownership, companion parrots*. Priority states: FL, CA, TX, NY, GA, CO, IL, AZ.
- **Budget to start:** $5–10/day per platform. Prove Meta first.
- **Objective:** Traffic or Leads (Meta) / "Drive conversions" or "Website traffic" (YouTube). Never a Sales/Shopping objective.
- **What success looks like:** clicks to the site + form inquiries (set up the /contact-us/ lead event — @cag-site-hygiene-agent already tracks `generate_lead`).
- **Never in an ad:** a specific bird's price, "buy now," gift-card/Zelle language, the 🦜 emoji, anything implying wild-caught.
