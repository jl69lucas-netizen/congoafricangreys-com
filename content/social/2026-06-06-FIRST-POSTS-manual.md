## PART 1 — Which platform gets which intent (and why)

| Platform | First-post intent | Framework | Angle | Ad-ready? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Facebook** | **Transactional / Commercial** | **PAS** (Problem→Agitate→Solve) | Scam-fear → documented trust → buy | ✅ Best ad platform (your 40–65 buyers live here) |
| **Instagram** (feed) | **Commercial** | **AIDA** (Attention→Interest→Desire→Action) | "Meet the flock that's available now" | ✅ via Meta (same as FB) |
| **Instagram** (Reel) | Entertainment → soft commercial | Hook→Story→CTA | Maxy talking | ⚠️ keep organic |
| **Pinterest** | **Comparison / Informational** | **QAB** (Question→Answer→Benefit) | "Congo or Timneh — which is right for you?" | ❌ evergreen search, not ads |
| **YouTube** | **Informational / Trust** | **H-S-S** (Hook→Story→Solution) | "What hand-tame actually looks like" | ❌ research intent, not direct sale |
| **TikTok** | **Entertainment** | Hook→3 beats→Follow | Maxy talking | ❌ audience too young to buy |

**Rule of thumb:** transactional copy = **Facebook + Instagram feed** (where buyers + ad money work). Everything else = trust/education/comparison that *feeds* those two. Don't run "for sale" as your first TikTok/YouTube — it underperforms and (for ads) risks rejection (see Part 3).

## PART 3 — Read this before you spend on ADS

⚠️ **Meta (Facebook/Instagram) restricts ads for the sale of live animals.** A boosted post that says "buy this bird, $2,500" can get **rejected or the account flagged**. This is the single most important ad caveat for a breeder.

**Safe ad strategy (when you're ready to spend):**
- **Boost the TRUST angle, not the price.** Run the Facebook PAS post (or a "how to spot a real breeder" version) as the ad — it drives to the website, where the actual transaction happens *off-platform*. That's policy-safe AND it's your best converter.
- **Where the money goes furthest:** Facebook/Instagram (Meta) ads to a 40–65, US, "parrot owner / African grey" interest audience. Skip YouTube/TikTok ads for a small budget.
- **Objective:** "Traffic" or "Leads" to /african-grey-parrot-for-sale/ — not "Sales/Catalog" (which expects e-commerce checkout + triggers the animal-sale filter).

So: **organic posts can be transactional (show prices). Paid ads should be trust-led → website.**
