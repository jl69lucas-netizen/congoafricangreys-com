🤔 FAQs – Everything You Need to Know

1. How Much Does a Male African Grey Parrot Cost?

The price of a male African Grey varies depending on age, training, and breeder reputation. At C.A.Gs, our hand-raised, trained parrots typically cost between $1,600 – $5,000.

2. Are Male African Grey Parrots More Talkative?

Male African Greys tend to start mimicking sounds and speech earlier, but both males and females are excellent talkers.

3. How Can I Train My Male African Grey to Talk?

-Start with simple words and use repetition.

-Use positive reinforcement (treats and praise).

-Interact daily to build vocabulary and confidence.

4. Do Male African Grey Parrots Need a Companion?

African Greys are social birds and thrive on human interaction. However, if you’re away often, consider getting a parrot companion.

5: Is It Legal to Own a Male African Grey in New York City, NY?

Yes, but African Greys are CITES-protected, so always buy from a licensed breeder.

6. How Long Do Male African Grey Parrots Live?

With proper care, an African Grey can live up to 50+ years in captivity. A well-balanced diet, mental stimulation, and regular vet check-ups are crucial for longevity.
