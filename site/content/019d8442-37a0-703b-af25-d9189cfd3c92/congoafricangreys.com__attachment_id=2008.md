## **Health & Common Conditions of African Grey Parrots**

At **C.A.Gs**, the health of our parrots is our top priority. Our Congo African Grey parrots undergo comprehensive health screenings before being made available for sale, ensuring they are in excellent condition.

### **Common African Grey Health Issues**

- Feather Plucking is often a result of boredom or stress, which can be minimized through proper stimulation and enrichment.
- [**Psittacosis**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Psittacosis+(Parrot+Fever)&oq=Psittacosis+(Parrot+Fever)&gs_lcrp=EgZjaHJvbWUyBggAEEUYOdIBCDE0NTNqMGoxqAIAsAIA&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8&sei=FJKnZ5GSAZ2uhbIP7sKC6Ac)(Parrot Fever) is a bacterial infection; all our birds are rigorously tested to prevent this condition.
- Calcium Deficiency: Managed through a balanced diet supplemented with calcium to ensure strong bones and feathers.
- Respiratory Infections: Preventable with a clean, well-ventilated environment and regular health check-ups.

### **African Grey Diet & Nutrition**
