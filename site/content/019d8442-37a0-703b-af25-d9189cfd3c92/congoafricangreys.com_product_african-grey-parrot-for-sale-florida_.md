## 🔹 FAQs – Buying an African Grey Parrot Sale Online

**❓ How much does an African Grey Parrot cost?**

❓ **Do you offer shipping?**

❓ **What is included with my purchase?**

✔ A spacious cage, food starter pack, vet records, and care guide.

**❓A Bird Sitting on My Shoulder – Is It Safe for My Parrot to Perch on My Shoulder?**

✔ Yes, but with proper training and supervision. African Greys love perching on their owner’s shoulders, but keep in mind:

✔ They may bite if startled – Train them to step up and down on command.

✔ Avoid allowing dominant behavior – If your parrot becomes aggressive, redirect them to a perch.

✔ Shoulder privileges should be earned – Only allow well-behaved, trained parrots on your shoulder.

💡 Always be mindful of body language to prevent sudden biting or unwanted behavior.

**❓At What Age Do African Greys Start Talking?**

✔ Most African Greys start mimicking sounds between 3-6 months and begin forming words by 12 months. By 2-3 years old, they can develop an extensive vocabulary and even understand context and emotions in speech.

💡 Repetition and interaction are key to helping your parrot learn and talk faster.

**❓Do African Grey Parrots Need a Lot of Attention?**

✔ Yes, African Greys are highly intelligent and social birds that require:

✔ Daily interaction (at least 2-3 hours of social time).

✔ Mental stimulation (toys, puzzles, and talking practice).

✔ Training & Bonding to prevent boredom and behavioral issues.

💡 If you have a busy lifestyle, consider getting two parrots for companionship or ensuring your bird has enough enrichment.
