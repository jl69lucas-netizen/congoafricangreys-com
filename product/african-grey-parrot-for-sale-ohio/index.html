🦜 About the African Grey Parrot

African Grey parrots belong to the Psittacidae family and are native to the rainforests of Central and West Africa.

✔ Scientific Classification:

  • Genus: Psittacus
  • Species: P. Erithacus
  • Order: Psittaciformes
  • Class: Aves
  • Lifespan: Up to 60 years in captivity

Congo African Grey vs. Timneh African Grey

  • Congo African Grey Parrot – Larger, with bright red tail feathers and a light grey body.
  • Timneh African Grey Parrot – Smaller, darker grey, with maroon tail feathers.
