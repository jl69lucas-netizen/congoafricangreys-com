# Design.md for Congo African Greys - Mobile Immersive Variant

## Brand Identity
A premium, trustworthy family aviary specializing in African Grey parrots. The mobile version focuses on touch-first interaction and high-contrast legibility for on-the-go browsing, while maintaining the brand's established trust and warmth.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green (#2D5A43)**: Solid dark green for headers, containers, and text contrast.
- **Secondary Orange (#E67451)**: High-visibility call-to-action color for primary buttons and text highlights.
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF)**: Primary text color on dark backgrounds.
- **Background Image**: Full-bleed background of African Grey parrots in a naturalistic home environment.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display) for classic, authoritative branding.
- **Body & Buttons**: Sans-serif font (e.g., Inter) for modern, clean readability on mobile screens.

## Mobile-Specific Visual Style
- **Full-Screen Immersive Background**: Uses a vertical crop of the aviary imagery.
- **Stacked Layout**: Vertical arrangement of content (Headline -> Subhead -> CTAs -> Badges) for optimal mobile scrolling.
- **Bottom Navigation**: Persistent tab bar (Home, Birds, About, Contact) for easy thumb access.
- **Card Badges**: Credential icons arranged in a grid or scrollable row for compact space management.
- **Large Tap Targets**: Buttons are sized for easy interaction on mobile devices.

## Layout Components
1. **Mobile Header**: Centered brand logo or name.
2. **Hero Section**: Immersive image with a dark green overlay card containing the primary messaging.
3. **Stacked CTAs**: Vertical button stack for "See Available Birds" and "Inquire Now."
4. **Credential Grid**: 2x2 or 1x4 grid of outlined icons for USDA, CITES, DNA, and Health certificates.
5. **Tab Bar Navigation**: Standard mobile bottom navigation.
