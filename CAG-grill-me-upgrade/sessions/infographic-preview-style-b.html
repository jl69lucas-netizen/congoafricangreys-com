African Grey Breeding Journey From clutch to your home — what to expect
CAG · TAG
📅 Total journey: 5–6 months from first egg to weaned, socialized bird ready for adoption.
🥚
Clutch Laid Day 0
2–4 eggs laid, 2–3 days apart. Incubation begins immediately by both parents.
🔆
Incubation Period 28 days
Eggs candled at day 7 and 14 to confirm fertility. Humidity and temperature monitored daily.
🐣
First Hatch Week 4
Chick pips and hatches over 24–48 hrs. Pin feathers emerge at 2–3 weeks. Eyes open at 3 weeks.
🍼
Hand-Feeding & Socialization 8–12 wks
Fed 3–4× daily. Handled from day 14 to build human bond. Vocabulary exposure begins.
✅
Weaned & Ready 12–16 wks
DNA sexed, avian vet cleared, CITES documentation issued. Available to approved homes.
