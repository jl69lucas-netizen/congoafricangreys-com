Recommendation

★ Option C — Floating Progress Sidebar

The sidebar rail is the strongest UX choice for a long-form 25-section homepage. It shows every section at a glance, tells the reader where they are at all times (green = read, clay = current), and never competes with content space. On mobile it collapses to a compact dot-only progress indicator at the top. It's also an SEO trust signal — organized, navigable pages reduce bounce rate.

Option A (sticky pills) is the simplest to build but costs vertical screen space on every scroll. Option B (section footer arrows) is great for linear readers but doesn't give random-access across 25 sections. Option C does both.
