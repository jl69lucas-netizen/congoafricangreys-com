## 1️⃣ Why Proper Nutrition Matters for African Grey Parrots

African Greys are intelligent parrots that require a nutrient-rich diet to support:

✔ Cognitive Development & Speech Abilities 🧠🗣️

✔ Feather Health & Color Enhancement 🌿🦜

✔ Strong Bones & Immune System 💪🔬

A well-balanced diet consists of:

✔ High-quality pellets – 60-70% of the diet

✔ Fresh Vegetables – 20-30%

✔ Fruits & Treats – 5-10%

✔ Protein & Healthy Fats – Small portions

🐦 **Did you know?** African Greys are **foraging birds**, meaning they love to search for food in creative ways. **[Learn about Parrot Intelligence & Foraging from National Geographic](https://www.nationalgeographic.com/animals/birds/facts/african-gray-parrot)**.

### 🥦 Bird Food: Vegetables – Essential Vitamins & Fiber

Fresh vegetables boost the immune system and enhance feather health.

✔ **Best Vegetables for African Greys:**

- Dark Leafy Greens (Kale, Collard Greens, Swiss Chard)
- Bell Peppers 🌶️
- Squash & Sweet Potatoes 🍠
- Carrots & Green Beans 🥕

🚨 **Avoid dressing & seasonings** – Birds **don’t need added salt, sugar, or oils**!

### 🍓 Bird Food: Fruits – Natural Antioxidants

Fruits should be given in **small portions** and **only a few times per week**.

✔ **Best Fruits for African Greys:**

- Mangoes 🥭
- Papayas 🍈
- Berries (Blueberries, Strawberries, Raspberries) 🍓

🚨 **Avoid acidic fruits like tomatoes**, as they may cause ulcers.

## 3️⃣ Foods African Greys Should NEVER Eat

Certain foods are **toxic** to African Greys and should always be avoided:

🚫 **Avocado** – Causes heart failure

🚫 **Onions & Garlic** – Damages red blood cells

🚫 **Chocolate & Caffeine** – Can be fatal

🚫 **Tomatoes & Mushrooms** – May cause ulcers

🚫 **Celery Strings** – Risk of crop impaction

🚫 **Dairy & Alcohol** – Birds cannot digest them

🐦 **Did you know?** Most avian vets recommend avoiding raw tomatoes due to their acidity.

## 4️⃣ Feeding Portions & Schedule

✔ **Small Birds (Cockatiels, Parakeets):**

- 1/4 cup pellets daily
- 1 teaspoon of fruits & vegetables

✔ **Larger Birds (African Greys, Amazons, Macaws):**

- 1/2 cup pellets daily
- 1 tablespoon of fruits & vegetables

🕒 **Feeding Schedule:**

- Morning: Pellets & fresh veggies
- Afternoon: Fruits or healthy treats
- Evening: Protein-rich foods & nuts
