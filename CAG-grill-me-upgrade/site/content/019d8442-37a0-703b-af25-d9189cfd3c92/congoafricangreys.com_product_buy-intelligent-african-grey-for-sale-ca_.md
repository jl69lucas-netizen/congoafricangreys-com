## Frequently Asked Questions About African Grey Parrots

### **❓What is the lifespan of an African Grey parrot?**

✔️ African Grey parrots typically live for 40 to 60 years in captivity, and some individuals have been known to live even longer with proper care. Providing a stimulating environment, a nutritious diet, and regular veterinary care are crucial factors in their longevity.

### **❓Are African Grey parrots good talkers?**

✔️ Yes, African Grey parrots are renowned for their exceptional ability to mimic human speech and sounds. With consistent training, they can develop impressive vocabularies and even use words in context. Their ability to associate words with meanings makes them fascinating communicators.

### **❓What do African Grey parrots eat?**

✔️ A balanced diet for an African Grey parrot consists of high-quality formulated pellets, a variety of fresh fruits and vegetables, and occasional nuts and seeds. It’s important to avoid toxic foods such as avocado, chocolate, and caffeine.

[![Safe Seeds and Nuts in African Grey Diet & Nutrition](https://congoafricangreys.com/wp-content/uploads/african-grey-parrot-seeds-nuts.jpg)](https://congoafricangreys.com/male-african-gray-for-sale-chicago-il/davinci/)

### ❓Do African Grey parrots require a lot of attention?

✔️ Yes, African Grey parrots are brilliant and social birds that require significant daily interaction and mental stimulation to thrive. Neglecting their social and mental needs can lead to behavioral problems such as feather plucking and excessive screaming.
