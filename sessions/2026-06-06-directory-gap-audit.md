## Executive summary

I audited where the 30 competitors are listed off-site, to find directories C.A.Gs is
missing. **Three patterns emerged — only one is worth copying:**

1. **Legit local/business directories (THE GAP — copy this).** The more credible-looking
   breeder, Afro Birds Farm, is cited on **Yelp, MapQuest, hub.biz, ZoomInfo, Pinterest,
   Tumblr, Facebook**. These general local/business citations are exactly what C.A.Gs lacks.
2. **Blog-comment spam (do NOT copy).** Silvergate Bird Farm's backlinks are comments dumped
   on unrelated blogs — a squash guide, a garden-pond post, a chess blog, a pasta recipe.
   Black-hat link-building; recorded only as evidence.
3. **Open classifieds (submit here).** Hoobly, BirdsNow, Petzlover, PetClassifieds, and
   BirdBreeders are themselves Tier-2 competitors AND open, mostly-free submission targets.

**Trust angle confirmed:** multiple competitors are flagged as scams — Silvergate on
**petscams.com**, Afro Birds Farm in a **ParrotForums "CAG scams"** thread, Exotic Parrot
Pet Store in "is it legit?" Facebook threads. C.A.Gs' documented, USDA-AWA/CITES,
trust-led positioning is a genuine differentiator on exactly the platforms (Yelp, BBB,
forums) where buyers vet breeders.
