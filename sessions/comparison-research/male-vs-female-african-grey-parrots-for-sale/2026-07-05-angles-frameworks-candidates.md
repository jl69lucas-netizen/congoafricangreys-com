## A. Core positioning (the one-line premise)
**The honest, transactional answer to "male or female?" — from breeders who DNA-sex every bird.** Competitors state sex-behavior folklore as fact and tell you to "go get a blood test." We say the truth (sex is a *weak* predictor; hand-rearing + individual temperament dominate) **and** hand you a DNA-sexed, documented bird. Honesty IS the conversion lever here.

## B. Trust angles (transactional page → trust is the spine)
1. **(Recommended) "DNA-sexed, not guessed."** — Every C.A.Gs bird ships with a PCR DNA-sexing certificate; visual sexing is folklore (Reddit: "look 100% identical… you need a blood test"). *Why:* directly answers the #1 SERP intent (sexing) + turns our standard doc into the differentiator. *Trade-off:* leans on DNA cert being shown on every listing — must stay inside the Verified-Claim Ledger (PCR sexing IS assertable).
2. **Honesty-over-hype.** — We tell you sex is a weak behavior predictor when every competitor sells the myth. *Why:* non-commodity moat + E-E-A-T; builds the trust that closes a 40–60-yr purchase. *Trade-off:* slightly undercuts the "pick a sex" urgency — resolve by pivoting urgency to *availability* (below), not sex.
3. **Documentation stack** — CITES Appendix I paperwork, hatch cert, avian-vet exam, closed band. *Why:* scam-fear intent (Reddit "legitimate website to purchase?"). *Trade-off:* overlaps other pages — keep tight, link out.
4. **Scam-safe payment counter-position** — parrothaven pushes PayPal Friends&Family / Skrill (buyer-unprotected). We name safe process. *Why:* live competitor example; converts scam-fearful buyers. *Trade-off:* must stay factual, not name-competitor-negatively on-page.

## C. Urgency angles (honest, not fake-scarcity)
1. **(Recommended) Real-availability urgency** — "Here's who's actually sexed and available right now" (live `/available/` sexed birds). *Why:* honest, ties urgency to inventory not to sex-myth; drives the money click. *Trade-off:* needs current clutch data at build time.
2. **Weaning/clutch timing** — sexed chicks reserved before weaning (12–16 wks). *Why:* genuine seasonal driver. *Trade-off:* don't imply pressure to rush a lifetime decision.
3. **DNA-cert-included-now** — the sexing + docs are done, no waiting/lab trip. *Why:* removes buyer friction competitors leave open.
