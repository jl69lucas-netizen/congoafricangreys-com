## 4. Competitor Reverse Engineering

### 4.1 Wikipedia / National Geographic / The Spruce Pets (the encyclopedic anchors)
- Own "african grey parrot comparison" via species-facts authority (taxonomy, appearance, Congo vs Timneh size/color).
- **Gap:** pure facts, no buyer decision, no cross-species comparison matrix, no routing, no "which is right for you."

### 4.2 BirdTricks — "Must-Know Things That Set African Greys Apart From Other Parrots" (closest to a hub)

- **Why it ranks:** legacy authority + exact-intent title.
- **Gap:** one article, not a hub; no per-rival comparison, no spokes, no selector, no variant/gender breakdown.

- Interactive selectors that Reddit/forums link to; own the decision-intent SERP.
- **Gap:** generic all-species tools, not grey-focused, don't route to real comparison content. A grey-centric selector that outputs "read the Congo-vs-Timneh spoke" fills the gap.
